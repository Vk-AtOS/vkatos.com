Frequently Asked Questions

What is the setup fee for?

The setup fee covers: configuring your connectors from your PRD, installing and configuring VKatOS on your server, training your team on the portal, and a 30-day hypercare period where we fix any issues immediately.

How long does setup take?

Most clients are live within 1–3 business days. You fill in a PRD (Product Requirements Document) form describing your process, and we configure the platform to match it exactly.

Do I need to host it myself?

VKatOS runs on your own machine or server — keeping your client data entirely in your control. We can also deploy it on a cloud VM for an additional hosting fee. We never store your business data on our servers.

What industries do you support?

Any industry with a repeatable process: education consultancy, recruitment, logistics, legal, healthcare, real estate, finance, insurance, manufacturing, and more. If your process can be described in steps, VKatOS can automate it.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — we offer a live demo on your actual use case. Book a 30-minute demo and we'll show you VKatOS running a pipeline similar to yours, with real data.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, bank transfer, and cards via Razorpay. GST invoices provided automatically. Monthly subscription billing is fully automated.
