Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. What data we collect

We collect information necessary to automate your business pipelines, which may include:

  • Contact information (Names, email addresses, phone numbers) of your team and clients.
  • Operational data (Pipeline stages, case details, deadlines).
  • Documents uploaded to the platform for processing (Passports, bank statements, etc.).
  • Communication logs between your team and your clients.

2. How we use it

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the VKatOS automation services.
  • Generate GAIA morning briefings.
  • Trigger automated notifications and communications.
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI models (anonymised only).

3. Data storage

All data is stored on secure servers located within India. We employ industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. Access to production data is strictly controlled and audited.

4. Data retention

We retain operational data as long as your account is active. Specifically, case-related documents and sensitive data are purged 90 days after case closure to ensure minimal data footprint while maintaining auditability.

5. Your rights under DPDP Act 2023

As a data fiduciary/processor operating in India, we strictly adhere to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct or erase your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Appoint a Data Manager for your organisation.

6. AI usage

VKatOS uses GAIA, our proprietary AI engine, to process your data. All prompts sent to GAIA are anonymised. Your sensitive business data is never used to train models for other clients without explicit, aggregated anonymisation.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at velan@vkatos.com.
