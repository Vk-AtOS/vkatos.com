Study Abroad Consultancy Automation

Never miss a university intake deadline again. Our autonomous pipelines manage document tracking, deadline intelligence, and visa processing for global consultancies.

Consultancy Pain Points

Missed Intake Deadlines

A single missing document can delay a student's application by 6 months. Manual tracking across hundreds of universities is prone to human error.

Document Follow-up Chaos

Counsellors spend 60% of their day asking students for transcripts, SOPs, and financial records via WhatsApp and Email.

Visa Pipeline Blindness

No real-time visibility into the status of visa applications, leading to last-minute panics and missed travel dates.

The VKatOS Fix

Deadline Intelligence

DCSP tracks university deadlines and sets autonomous triggers based on document completion status, ensuring submissions happen weeks in advance.

Autonomous Doc Tracking

Aether2 identifies missing documents and automatically nudges students via their preferred channel, validating uploads in real time using AI.

Visa Watchdog

Velaris provides a dedicated visa tracker that identifies stalls in the consulate pipeline, allowing for immediate administrative intervention.
