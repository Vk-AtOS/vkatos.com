Built for Manufacturing Teams

Features built for
production operations

Real-time order tracking

Every order at every stage, updated by the floor team via WhatsApp. No new software for your workers. No training. Just reply to a WhatsApp message to update the stage.

Delay prediction

GAIA calculates Z-scores across your production data. If an order is trending late, you know 3 days before the deadline — time to add resources or manage the customer.
