// architecture

Your data stays
in your network.

Connector-based architecture. We never touch your data. Your IT team installs a lightweight connector — that's the only access we need.

01

Connector installed on your server

Lightweight Python package. Your IT team installs it inside your network. Source code available for security review. Takes 30 minutes.

02

Connector strips all PII locally

Before any signal leaves your network, PII is removed. Only structured data — stage, score, action-needed — is transmitted. Nothing sensitive ever leaves.

03

Axium platform processes and acts

Our platform receives the signal, runs the AI scoring and routing, detects stalls, generates actions, and sends them back to your connector.

04

Actions execute inside your network

Notifications, alerts, document requests, offer generation — all execute within your infrastructure. Full audit trail. Tamper-proof log. Zero external data exposure.
