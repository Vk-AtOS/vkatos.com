How It Works

From first contact to
final invoice — automated

VKatOS connects every stage of your business into one intelligent pipeline.

1

Intake

A new enquiry, CV, document, or order arrives. VKatOS captures it from WhatsApp, email, web form, or API — and classifies it in seconds.

2

Score & Route

GAIA and Aether2 (our multi-agent AI) score the intake, match it to your pipeline rules, and automatically advance — or escalate to the right person.

3

Execute & Notify

Approved actions run. The customer gets a WhatsApp update with a live case tracker. Your staff sees what changed and what's needed next.

4

Monitor & Predict

GAIA watches every stage 24/7. If a case sits too long, it alerts you before the deadline — or auto-escalates based on your rules.

5

Invoice & Report

At the end of each cycle, VKatOS auto-generates GST invoices, daily briefings, and P&L reports — no accountant needed for routine work.

Platform Features

Everything your business
needs to run itself

🧠

GAIA — AI Business Brain

Autonomous heartbeat runs every 15 minutes. Monitors health scores, generates daily briefings, repairs broken stages, and escalates failures before they impact clients.

⚡

Pipeline Automation

Map your entire business process as a connector. VKatOS auto-advances cases, scores documents via OCR, routes to the right staff, and logs every action immutably.

📱

WhatsApp-Native

Customers get updates at every stage. Staff receive alerts for pending actions. Reply "1" to approve, "2" to reject — all without logging in.

🔒

Immutable Audit Trail

SHA-256 hashed event log of every action, every decision, every escalation. DPDP-compliant data handling. One-click compliance reports.

📊

Financial Intelligence

MRR, ARR, P&L, GST summaries, cash flow, and liability tracking — all live. Auto-generate GST invoices with your branding. Razorpay billing built-in.

🔗

Any Industry, Any Pipeline

Education consultancy, recruitment, logistics, healthcare, legal, real estate — describe your process in plain English and VKatOS builds the connector in minutes.

Self-Healing System

DCSP detects anomalies using Z-score analysis. Aether2 orchestrates repair agents. Most failures are fixed automatically — before you even know they happened.

Every customer gets a unique tracking link. No login required. Live stage progress, estimated days, next action — on any phone, in any language.
