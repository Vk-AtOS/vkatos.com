GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE SETUP — DO THIS ONCE
Takes 15 minutes. Do it as soon as the website is live.

STEP 1: Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
  - Sign in with your Google account
  - Click "Add Property"
  - Select "Domain" and enter: vkatos.com
  - Click Continue

STEP 2: Verify domain ownership via Namecheap DNS
  - Google shows you a TXT record value like:
    google-site-verification=xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
  - Log in to Namecheap at namecheap.com
  - Go to Domain List → vkatos.com → Manage
  - Click "Advanced DNS" tab
  - Click "Add New Record"
  - Type: TXT Record
  - Host: @
  - Value: paste the google-site-verification value here
  - TTL: Automatic
  - Click the green checkmark to save
  - Go back to Google Search Console and click "Verify"

STEP 3: Submit your sitemap
  - In Search Console left menu: Sitemaps
  - Enter: sitemap.xml
  - Click Submit

STEP 4: Request indexing for homepage
  - In Search Console: URL Inspection at top
  - Enter: https://vkatos.com
  - Click "Request Indexing"

STEP 5: Add meta verification tag to index.html
  - Replace PASTE_YOUR_CODE_HERE in index.html with your actual code
